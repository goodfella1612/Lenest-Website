Laparoscopy

Advanced, Minimally Invasive Surgery

At Le Nest, we offer state-of-the-art laparoscopic (keyhole) surgery for a variety of conditions, providing patients with a safer, less invasive alternative to traditional open surgery. Our skilled surgeons use specialized instruments and cameras to perform precise procedures through tiny incisions, resulting in faster recovery, less pain, and minimal scarring.

Our Laparoscopic Services

Gynaecological Laparoscopy

Diagnosis and treatment of fibroids, ovarian cysts, endometriosis, and infertility.

General Surgery

Gallbladder removal, appendectomy, hernia repair, and more.

Shorter Hospital Stay

Most procedures are done as day-care or require minimal hospitalization.

Quick Recovery

Return to normal activities sooner with less discomfort.
