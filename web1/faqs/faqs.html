FAQs

1) How do I book an appointment at Le Nest? +

You can book an appointment by calling us at +91 7045340141 or +91 8104516661, or by clicking the "BOOK AN APPOINTMENT" button on our website. Our staff will help you schedule a convenient time that fits your needs.

2) Is fasting required for my test? +

Fasting requirements depend on the specific test. For most blood tests like glucose levels, lipid profiles, and some hormone tests, 8-12 hours of fasting may be required. We'll inform you of any specific requirements when you book your appointment.

3) Are all the Doctors available round-the-clock? +

Our regular clinic hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. However, we have emergency services available 24/7 for urgent medical situations. Emergency cases are handled by our on-call medical team.

4) What are the services available at Le Nest? +

We offer comprehensive healthcare services including Gynaecology & Obstetric Care, Pediatric Care, Physiotherapy, Hypnobirthing, Antenatal Classes, and Relationship Enhancement Programs. We also provide labor services like kick count calculators and contraction monitoring.

5) Do you have the facility of sample pickup for diagnostics? +

Yes, we offer home sample collection services for certain diagnostic tests. Please contact our lab department at +91 7045340141 to schedule a home pickup and confirm which tests are available for this service.

6) What are the working hours? Are you open on Sundays? +

Our regular hours are Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on weekends (Saturday and Sunday). However, emergency services are available 24/7 for urgent medical needs.

7) Can emergencies be treated at Le Nest? +

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency care for obstetric, gynecological, and pediatric emergencies. Our emergency team is equipped to handle urgent situations. For life-threatening emergencies, please call immediately or visit our emergency department.

8) Do you organize camps? +

Yes, we regularly organize health camps and awareness programs in the community. These include prenatal education camps, women's health screenings, and pediatric health camps. Follow our social media or contact us for information about upcoming camps.

9) Can I get my child over for immunization? +

Absolutely! We provide complete pediatric immunization services following the standard vaccination schedule. Our pediatric team will ensure your child receives all necessary vaccines at the appropriate ages. Please bring your child's immunization card during visits.
